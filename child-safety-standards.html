Child Safety Standards

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This document outlines the child safety standards for Moments - Aktivteter ("Moments"), a social activity app developed and operated by Lemon App AS, Oslo, Norway.

Zero Tolerance Policy: Moments has a zero tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or activity that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors is strictly prohibited on our platform and will result in immediate action, including account termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

Age Requirement

Moments is intended exclusively for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly allow users under the age of 18 to create accounts or use our services. Users must confirm they are at least 18 years old during the registration process.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following is strictly prohibited on Moments:

  • Any content that depicts, promotes, or glorifies child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE)
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind, including AI-generated material
  • Any attempt to groom, solicit, or exploit minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting exploitative content involving minors
  • Any behavior that endangers the safety or well-being of children
  • Using the platform to contact or engage with minors for inappropriate purposes

Enforcement

When we identify or receive reports of CSAE content or behavior, we take immediate action:

  • Immediate removal of the offending content
  • Immediate suspension or permanent ban of the offending account
  • Reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or relevant local law enforcement authorities, including Norwegian police (Kripos)
  • Preservation of evidence for law enforcement as required by law

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior on Moments that involves or endangers children, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: Use the report function available on any user profile or in any chat conversation
  • Email: Contact us at michael.ray@momentsapp.no

All reports are reviewed promptly. Reports involving child safety are prioritized and escalated immediately.

Prevention Measures

Moments implements the following measures to protect against CSAE:

  • Age verification during account registration (minimum age: 18)
  • User reporting tools accessible throughout the app
  • Content moderation and review of reported accounts
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies on all child safety matters
  • Regular review and updates to our safety practices

Compliance

Moments complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • Norwegian criminal law regarding child exploitation
  • EU regulations on combating child sexual abuse
  • Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy
  • Applicable international laws and conventions on child protection
