Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Moments ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Profile information (photo, bio, interests)
  • Location data (when you grant permission)
  • Device information (OS version, device type)

Usage Data

  • Events you view or join
  • Messages you send (content not stored permanently)
  • App interactions and preferences
  • Analytics data for app improvement

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To match you with relevant events and people
  • To send notifications about events you've joined
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To communicate with you about updates or issues

Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase for secure data storage. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Expo: Push notifications and analytics
  • Mixpanel: Analytics (optional, can be disabled)
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication option

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Location Data

We collect location data only when you explicitly grant permission. This is used to show you nearby events. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as you have an active account. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it.

Children's Privacy

Moments is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
